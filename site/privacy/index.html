Privacy Policy

How Sidekick handles data on your machine and on this website.

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Sidekick is designed as a local-first plugin: enforcement runs in your coding-agent session on your machine. We do not operate a Sidekick cloud backend that receives your source code, prompts, or session transcripts.

1. No telemetry from Sidekick

The Sidekick plugin does not include analytics or telemetry that reports your usage to Alo Labs. Optional tools you configure in your own environment are outside this policy unless you enable them.

2. Website (sidekick.alolabs.dev)

This static site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most websites, the hosting provider may process standard server logs (such as IP address, user agent, and requested URL) for security and operations. We do not use the Sidekick site to build advertising profiles or sell personal data.

3. Local storage in your browser

The site may store your theme preference (light or dark) in localStorage on your device. Help Center search may index public documentation in your browser for offline-style lookup. This data stays on your device unless you clear it.

4. Third-party coding agents

When you use Sidekick inside Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, or another host, that host’s privacy policy governs how your prompts and code are processed. Sidekick adds hooks and skills locally; it does not replace or override your host’s data practices. Kay and Codex child runtimes have their own policies as well.

5. Open source and GitHub

Source code and issues are published on GitHub. If you contribute or open issues, GitHub’s terms and privacy policy apply to information you submit there.

6. Children

The site and software are intended for developers and engineering teams. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the product or hosting changes. The “Last updated” date will reflect revisions.

8. Contact

Privacy questions may be directed to Alo Labs via alolabs.dev or the sidekick repository.
